VectorFusion synthesizes SVGs in various styles based on text prompts. It supports the use of three vector primitives, including Iconography, Sketch and Pixel Art.

DiffSketcher: Text Guided Vector Sketch Synthesis through Latent Diffusion Models
(NIPS 2023)

Project
Live
Paper
Code

DiffSketcher synthesizes vector sketches based on text prompts. It also supports the optimization of stroke width and color. It can also integrate Style Transfer during synthesis process.
